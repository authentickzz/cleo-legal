Cleo Support

Need help with Cleo? We are here to help with subscriptions, account access, generated content, audio playback, and technical issues.

Contact support

Support requests are sent securely to Cleo's support backend. This page does not expose a personal email address.

In-app support

You can also contact support inside the app from Settings → Subscription → Contact support.

Subscriptions

Cleo subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel or manage a subscription, open the App Store account subscriptions page on your device.
