Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Netflow Soft LLP ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Cleo mobile application ("App") and related services ("Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Cleo, you may provide the following information during onboarding and profile setup:

  • Personal details: name, age, city, career, gender, and sexuality
  • Relationships: information about close people and children you choose to share
  • Lifestyle preferences: tags describing your habits, interests, and topics to exclude
  • Free-text notes: additional context you provide in the "Anything to Know" section
  • Feedback and reports: information you submit through troubleshooting or support features

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: features you interact with, session duration, playback preferences
  • AI interaction data: your interactions with AI-generated stories and affirmations (used to improve personalization)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone without explicit permission
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your experience: generate AI-powered manifestation stories and affirmations tailored to your profile, relationships, and goals
  • Improve our Services: analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and develop new functionality
  • Provide support: respond to your inquiries and troubleshooting reports
  • Process payments: manage subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Ensure security: detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

3. AI-Generated Content

Cleo uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized stories and affirmations based on the information you provide. Please note:

  • Your profile data is used as context for AI content generation
  • AI-generated content is created in real-time and is unique to your experience
  • We may use anonymized and aggregated interaction data to improve AI model performance
  • We do not use your personal data to train AI models shared with third parties

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your profile data is currently stored locally on your device
  • When cloud features are introduced, data will be stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption
  • We use HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between the App and our servers
  • We implement access controls and regular security audits to protect your data

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following categories of third-party services:

  • AI providers: for generating personalized content (stories, affirmations, voice synthesis)
  • Analytics: to understand how users interact with the App
  • Payment processors: Apple App Store and Google Play Store handle all subscription payments — we do not directly process or store your payment information
  • Cloud infrastructure: for data storage and service hosting

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: request deletion of your personal data (available via "Delete Account" in Settings)
  • Portability: request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: opt out of certain data processing activities

For EU/UK Users (GDPR)

We process your data based on: (a) your consent, (b) performance of a contract (providing our Services), and (c) our legitimate interests (improving the App). You have the right to withdraw consent at any time and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California Users (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

For Kazakhstan Users

Your data is processed in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On Personal Data and Their Protection." You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cleo.app.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our Services. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Cleo is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
